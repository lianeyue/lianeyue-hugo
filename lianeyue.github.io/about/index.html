About

I believe in service leadership, in creating opportunities for myself and for others, and in embracing creative chances.

Right now, I consult with Ernst & Young to drive technology transformation through IT strategy, architecture and infrastructure. Before that, I managed ERP and CRM deployments at GE Healthcare and Telamon. I’ve worked across the healthcare, automotive and utilities industries (to name a few) and developed skills in UI/UX, project management, and supply chain process improvement. I’m known to team members as an effective, enthusiastic “ray of sunshine” who always stockpiles snacks to share.

On the road, I enjoy exchanging postcards, working on screenplays, and practicing my Chinese and French. When I’m home, I volunteer with resettled families at RefugeeOne.

Thanks for stopping by, and let’s connect! I’d love to meet a future friend, collaborator, or pen pal.
